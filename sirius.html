Sirius

Wind blows through the trees
The stars shift with the seasons
I will nap all day.
-- Meditations of Sirius

Sirius has achieved a level of Zen that many seek and few attain. He leads a simple life and lets unimportant things slide off his back as water slides from a duck.

Sirius has short, stumpy legs and a round belly, and his footsteps make a distinctive sound as he trots around the house. He is also a connoiseur of the most luxurious comfortable locations to curl up and sleep away the afternoon, such as the towel closet or Caitlin's office chair. He is perfectly happy staring into space without any focus until inevitably his eyes close and he falls asleep.

Sirius likes:

  • Snoozing in a comfy spot
  • Snacking on dry kibble
  • Cuddling on his terms
  • Doing yoga with Mom
  • Sitting under the Christmas tree

Sirius does not like:

  • Having to share his favorite comfy spots with Remus
  • Being put in his cat carrier

Trivia

Sirius is rarely referred to by his name, which is fine because he doesn't appear to know what his name is. Potch has compiled a list of names we call this sweet cat.
